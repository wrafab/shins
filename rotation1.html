Tower Challenge

Week 1 | The Tower Challenge

The assignment for the week was to create an enclosure for an Arduino Uno or Raspberry Pi using fusion 360, and document it on my Page. I chose to create an enclosure for the raspberry pi and it turned out a lot more diffuclut than I thought it was going to be. Follow the progress with pictures that are included below as well my files at the very bottom.

Fusion 360

These pictures reflect the first few steps that I took in this Venture (Images 1 and 2) | These are the finished designs for both the bottom and top half of my enclosure. The pillers coming out of the 4th picture is my design to close my box. (Images 3 and 4)


Sucesses and Failures

As of now, before seeing the final product in the material world, I will attempt to document the various sucesses and failure so far. When I first started this project I tried making one solid figure and working on that. My plan was to hollow that figure out and then create the various holes nessecary for the various outlets that were needed. This soon proved to be too difficult hence why i decided to create two seperate files for both the bottom and top half. I took the origanl solid figure, bisected with a plane, used the shell feature, and created a copy of that file. In this way I would be a hundred percent sure my box would fit together in the end. After this difficulty the rest of my construction proved to be pretty easy. My solution for closing this figure was to create semicircles on the corners of my figure and have a piller running through them all.
