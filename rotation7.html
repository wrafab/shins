Rotation 7

Creating my own Skateboard

Through using a vacuum bag,my objective for rotation 7 was to make my own skateboard and decorating it with stickers printed by Roland GS-24.

What did I learn?

From this rotation, I realized how simple it could be to make a skateboard of my own. Not only can I build skaeboard out of the wood, but I can also make any furnitures such as wooden chairs or desks by going through the same process as making the skateboard. Furthermore, I noticed that it was easy to smoothen the surface of a wood by utilizing the wood cutters. However, it was pretty hard for me to properly use the wood cutters. I actually ended up cracking the base of my skateboard. Yet, I was able to fix the crack by adding superglue and using the clamp.
