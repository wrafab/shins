Max Shin|Digital Fabrication and Engineering 2017

Rotation 4

3-D Printing a wax stamp

Through using Fusion360, my objective of rotation 2 was to design and 3-D print a wax stamp.

Designing the stamp

This is my finished product!

I coated my wax stamp with colors on Fusion360, but the 3-D printer was not able to paint the colors on it.


What did I learn?

During my second rotation, we had the opportunity to play around with Fustion360. I realized that I would be able to create anything that I want with using Fusion360 and the 3-D printer. When I first used the app, I was surprised by the fact that I can manipulate any object and print it out in real life. Furthermore, the fact that creating a real-life object on a computer really shook me. I learned how to make complex shapes on Fusion360 and measure the appropriate dimensions of an object. Overall, I am very pleased with my wax stamp!
