Rotation 3

Designing and printing my T-shirt

Using Microsoft photoshop, illustrator, PDIP, and the 3-D printer, my objective for rotation 3 was to design and print out my own T-shirt.

Designing the T-Shirt

This is my finished product!

I decided to put our dorm pictures on my T-shirt. People in North Hall are always energetic and they are like my family. I made this T-shirt in the hopes of this shirt being our dorm shirts for this year.


What did I learn?

By my third rotation, we had the opportunity to design our own T-shirts through using Microsoft Illustrator and Photoshop. It was important that we heated the T-shirt before printing the picture on it, since the picture does not come out well if it is wrinkled. One crucial thing that I learned was that we should not be printing out copyrighted materials. It was fascinating how we can imprint the pictures that we took and make a T-shirt with it!
